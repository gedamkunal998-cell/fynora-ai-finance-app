Fynora Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Welcome to Fynora – Your AI Finance Companion.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (optional)

Financial Information

To provide expense tracking and analytics, we may process:

  • Transactions added manually
  • Transaction SMS (only after you grant permission)
  • Receipt images uploaded by you
  • AI-generated expense categories and insights
  • Budgets and savings goals

Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • Diagnostic information

2. SMS Permission

Fynora requests SMS permission only after your explicit consent.

This permission is used exclusively to:

  • Detect bank transaction SMS
  • Extract transaction details such as amount, merchant, bank, and date
  • Automatically create expense records

We do not use SMS for:

  • Reading personal conversations
  • Marketing
  • Advertising
  • Selling user data

You can revoke SMS permission anytime from your device settings.

3. Camera & Photos

Camera and gallery access are used only to:

  • Scan receipts
  • Upload receipt images
  • Extract transaction details using AI

Receipt images are processed only to provide the requested feature.

4. AI Processing

Fynora uses AI to:

  • Categorize expenses
  • Generate spending insights
  • Analyze uploaded receipts
  • Provide financial recommendations

Only the minimum information required is processed.

5. Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed securely through Razorpay.

Fynora does not store:

  • Card numbers
  • UPI PINs
  • Banking passwords

Payment information is handled directly by Razorpay under its own privacy policy.

6. Data Storage

Your data may include:

  • Transactions
  • Categories
  • Budgets
  • Savings goals
  • AI insights
  • Account information

Reasonable security measures are used to protect your information.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Information may be shared only with trusted service providers required to operate the application, including:

  • Authentication services
  • Cloud infrastructure
  • AI processing services
  • Payment gateway providers

8. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. While no system is completely secure, we work to reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

9. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your data
  • Update your information
  • Delete your account
  • Request deletion of stored financial data

10. Children's Privacy

Fynora is not intended for children under the age of 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:
