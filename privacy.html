Fynora Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Welcome to Fynora - Your AI Finance Companion.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Photo (Optional)

Financial Information

  • Transactions added manually
  • Bank transaction SMS (only after permission)
  • Receipt images uploaded by you
  • AI-generated categories and insights
  • Budgets and Savings Goals

Device Information

  • Device Model
  • Operating System
  • Application Version
  • Crash Logs
  • Diagnostic Information

2. SMS Permission

Fynora requests SMS permission only after your explicit consent.

This permission is used only for:

  • Detecting bank transaction SMS
  • Extracting transaction amount, merchant, bank and date
  • Automatically creating expense records

We never use SMS for:

  • Reading personal conversations
  • Marketing
  • Advertising
  • Selling user data

3. Camera & Gallery

Camera and Gallery permissions are used only for:

  • Scanning receipts
  • Uploading receipts
  • Extracting receipt information using AI

4. AI Processing

Fynora uses AI to:

  • Categorize expenses
  • Generate financial insights
  • Analyze receipts
  • Provide spending recommendations

5. Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed securely through Razorpay.

Fynora never stores:

  • Card Numbers
  • UPI PIN
  • Bank Passwords

6. Data Sharing

We never sell or rent your data.

Information may only be shared with trusted service providers required for:

  • Authentication
  • Cloud Storage
  • AI Processing
  • Payment Processing

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your data
  • Update your information
  • Delete your account
  • Request deletion of financial information

9. Children's Privacy

Fynora is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
