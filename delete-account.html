Delete Your Fynora Account

Effective Date: June 2026

If you wish to permanently delete your Fynora account, you may request deletion at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to:

support@fynora.app

Subject:

Delete My Fynora Account

Please include the email address associated with your account.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your Profile
  • Your Transactions
  • Budgets
  • Savings Goals
  • AI Insights
  • Expense Categories
  • Account Preferences

What May Be Retained

Where required by applicable law, certain payment or financial records may be retained for legal, tax, fraud prevention, or regulatory compliance purposes.

Processing Time

Your deletion request will normally be processed within 7 business days.
