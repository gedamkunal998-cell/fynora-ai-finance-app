Delete Your Account

You have full control over your data. Deletion is permanent, transparent and free.

At Fynora, your data is yours — always. You can permanently delete your account and every piece of personal data we hold about you at any time.

How to request deletion

Send an email from the address associated with your Fynora account to:

To: fynorasupport@gmail.com
Subject: Delete My Account
Body (optional): Full name registered on Fynora. Reason for leaving (optional — helps us improve).

Email deletion request

What happens next

1. Verification (1–2 business days)

We verify that the deletion request came from the email registered on the account. We may ask you a security question or ask you to reply from within the app to prevent malicious deletion requests.

2. Permanent deletion (within 7 business days)

Once verified, your account and every associated record are permanently removed from our production systems, including:

  • Profile (name, email, avatar, preferences)
  • All transactions, budgets, goals, achievements and streaks
  • All uploaded receipts and OCR results
  • Session tokens, device tokens and notification preferences
  • AI insight cache and analytics attributed to your user id

3. Backup purge (within 30 days)

Encrypted rolling backups are also purged, after which recovery of any part of your data is technically impossible.

4. Confirmation email

You will receive a final confirmation email once deletion is complete. If you subscribed to Fynora Pro, any pending refund will be processed to your original payment method.

Important.

Deletion is permanent. Once complete we cannot recover any part of your account. If you only want to take a break, consider logging out or turning off notifications instead of deleting.

Before you go…

If something is broken or missing in Fynora, we’d love a chance to fix it. Email us at fynorasupport@gmail.com — we read every message.
