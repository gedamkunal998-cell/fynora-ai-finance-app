Delete Your Fynora Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Fynora account, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to fynorasupport@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Fynora Account
  3. Include the email address associated with your Fynora account.
  4. Our support team will verify your request.
  5. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User Account
  • Transactions
  • Budgets
  • Savings Goals
  • AI Insights
  • Profile Information

Data That May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where required by applicable laws, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or regulatory compliance.
