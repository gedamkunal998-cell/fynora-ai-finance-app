Features

AI Expense Tracking

Every transaction is automatically categorised into Food, Fuel, Shopping, Bills and more — using AI that learns your spending patterns.

Budget Planner

Set monthly limits per category. Fynora nudges you before you cross the line — never after.

Savings Goals

Emergency fund, vacation, new bike, dream laptop — visualise every goal with progress rings that celebrate every rupee saved.

Paste bank SMS or let Fynora parse them — every UPI, card and net-banking transaction turns into a clean entry instantly.

Security & Privacy

Built with paranoia,
so you don't have to be.

Your financial data is the most sensitive data you own. We treat it that way.

End-to-End Encryption

Every transaction, budget and goal is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest with AES-256. Only you can decrypt your data.

User Privacy First

We never sell your data. No advertising trackers. No dark patterns. Your data is used only to power features you asked for.

Secure Cloud Infrastructure

Hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure with 24/7 monitoring, hardened firewalls and continuous security audits.

AI Data Protection

Prompts sent to our AI models never include your personal identity. Data is not used to train third-party models.

About Fynora

An AI companion, not a
financial advisor.

Fynora is an AI-powered personal finance application built to help everyday Indians track expenses, create budgets, build savings and understand spending — clearly, calmly and in their own language.

We built Fynora because personal finance apps have been either bank-branded lock-ins or overwhelming spreadsheets. Fynora is neither. It is a thoughtful, private, AI-assisted place where your money finally makes sense.

Important — What Fynora is NOT.

Fynora does not provide any of the following services, and none of the information in the app should be taken as professional financial advice:

  • Investment advice or portfolio management
  • Stock trading or brokerage services
  • Lending, credit lines or loans
  • Insurance products
  • Cryptocurrency trading, exchange or wallets
  • Forex trading or currency exchange
